About Venus


Numbers:

  • One Day: 243 Earth days
  • One Year: 225 Earth days
  • High Surface Temp: 880 F
  • Radius: 3,760 miles

Facts:

  • Venus does not experience seasons, like most planets, because its axial tilt is only 3 degrees.
  • It rotates east to west instead of west to east like all other planets in our solar system, except Uranus.
  • It has an iron core, like Mercury and Earth, which causes volcanoes on its surface.
