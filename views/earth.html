About Earth


Numbers:

  • One Day: 23.9 hours
  • One Year: 365.25 days
  • High Surface Temp: 130 F
  • Low Surface Temp: -120 F
  • Radius: 3,959 miles

Facts:

  • 70 percent of the Earth's surface is covered in water.
  • It has an iron and nickel core.
  • The Earth's atmosphere is so powerful that it can burn up almost any meteoroid before it can collide with the surface of the earth.
  • The Earth's atmosphere also protects humans from the intense radiation from the sun.
