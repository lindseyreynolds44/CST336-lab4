About Mercury


Numbers:

  • One Day: 176 Earth days
  • One Year: 88 Earth days
  • High Surface Temp: 840 F
  • Low Surface Temp: -275 F
  • Radius: 1,516 miles

Facts:

  • Ice has been found on the north and south poles
  • Mercury is the smallest of all the planets in our solar system and is continuously shrinking.
  • Mercury has a magnetic field and and "iron core"
