Welcome to Mars!


About Mars


Numbers:

  • One Day: 24.6 hours
  • One Year: 687 Earth days
  • High Surface Temp: 70 F
  • Low Surface Temp: -225 F
  • Radius: 2,106 miles

Facts:

  • Like Earth, Mars has seasons. However, because of the way it orbits around the sun, some seasons are longer than others.
  • Its core is made up of not only iron, like Mercury and Earth, but also of nickel and sulfur.
  • On Mars' surface lies the "largest volcano in the solar system." It has been named Olympus Mons and is three times the size of Mount Everest.
